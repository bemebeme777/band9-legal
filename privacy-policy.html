Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

Band9 is an AI-powered IELTS Speaking trainer. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used. We are committed to handling your information with care.

1. Who We Are

Band9 ("we", "our", "the app") is an independent mobile application developed by Dima, an individual developer. You can contact us at deronthedar@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide during onboarding:

  • Current IELTS band score
  • Target IELTS band score
  • Exam date
  • Self-identified main weakness (e.g. vocabulary, fluency)

Voice recordings:

When you practice speaking, your voice is recorded on your device. The audio file is sent to our secure server solely to generate a text transcript (via OpenAI Whisper). We do not store your audio recordings. They are processed in real time and discarded immediately after transcription.

Practice session data:

  • Text transcripts of your spoken responses
  • AI-generated band scores and feedback per session
  • Session history (date, topic, score)
  • Streak and practice frequency
  • Vocabulary flashcard progress

Device & technical data:

  • Device type and operating system version (collected automatically by the app platform)
  • App crash reports (if you consent via device settings)

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information unless you contact us directly.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To transcribe your spoken responses and generate IELTS band scores and feedback
  • To personalise your practice experience (roadmap, weak area focus, AI coach responses)
  • To track your progress over time (streak, band trend, session history)
  • To improve the accuracy of our scoring model

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

4. Where Your Data Is Stored

All practice history, scores, and settings are stored locally on your device using your device's secure storage. This data does not leave your device except as described in Section 5 below.

If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is permanently deleted.

5. Third-Party Services

Band9 uses the following third-party services to deliver its core functionality:

OpenAI AI Processing
Your audio is transcribed using Whisper, and your text responses are scored using GPT-4o-mini. Audio and transcripts are sent to OpenAI servers for processing and are subject to OpenAI's data retention policies (inputs are not used to train OpenAI models by default via the API).

openai.com/privacy →
Supabase Secure Proxy
We route all AI requests through a Supabase Edge Function acting as a secure server-side proxy. This ensures your API credentials and audio data are never exposed on the client. Supabase processes requests in transit but does not store your audio or transcripts.

supabase.com/privacy →
RevenueCat Subscriptions
Subscription management (Pro plan) is handled by RevenueCat. RevenueCat may collect a non-identifiable device ID and purchase metadata to manage your subscription status. No payment card information passes through RevenueCat — payments are processed entirely by Apple App Store or Google Play.

revenuecat.com/privacy →
Apple App Store / Google Play Payments
Subscription payments are processed by Apple or Google depending on your device. Their respective privacy policies govern the payment transaction data.

apple.com/legal/privacy →    policies.google.com/privacy →

6. Data Retention

Audio recordings: Never stored. Processed in real time and discarded.

Transcripts and scores: Stored locally on your device until you delete the app or use the "Reset All Progress" feature in Settings.

Subscription data: Retained by RevenueCat and Apple/Google per their policies.

7. Children's Privacy

Band9 is intended for users aged 16 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your country of residence, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Data portability

Since most data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by using "Reset All Progress" in Settings, or by uninstalling the app. For any other requests, contact us at deronthedar@gmail.com.

9. Security

We take security seriously. All communication between the app and our servers uses HTTPS encryption. Your OpenAI API key is stored exclusively as a server-side secret and is never exposed to the client application. Audio data is transmitted over encrypted connections and immediately discarded after processing.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of Band9 after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
